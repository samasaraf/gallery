Ballast Point Sculpin IPA is a trophy beer that s a testament to Ballast Point's homebrew roots. Sculpin IPA's inspired use of hops creates hints of apricot, peach, mango, and lemon flavors, but still pks a bit of a sting, just like a Sculpin fish. International Bitterness Units (IBUs) = 70/100. Craft beer brewed in San Diego, California since 1996. Best served chilled.

Product Description

California- American IPA- 7% ABV. Our Unfiltered Sculpin IPA packs hops into every sip, enhancing the classic Sculpin notes of fresh apricot, peach, mango and lemon with a slight haze and less bitterness. Freshness is key to every beer, but especially true for this extra-hopped IPA.
